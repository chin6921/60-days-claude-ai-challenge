💡 Success Tips for Students

Start Small, Think Big
Don't try to build everything at once. Each week builds on the last. Focus on ONE thing per week.
Experiment > Perfect
There's no "perfect" prompt. Iterate, test, learn from failures. Messy experiments teach more than perfection.
Write It Down
Document what works and why. This builds your understanding and creates shareable portfolio pieces.
Use Your Communication Strength
Your communication background is an asset! Think about how to explain complex ideas clearly to AI.
Share Your Learning
Post on social media, write a blog, or just explain to friends. Teaching others solidifies your knowledge.
Stick to the Schedule
3 hours/week is realistic. Block it in your calendar. Consistency beats cramming.
