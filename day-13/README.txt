# 🤖 AI Job Search Assistant

## 📖 About the Project

The AI Job Search Assistant is a prompt-engineered solution designed to make job hunting more efficient and personalized. Instead of manually browsing multiple job portals, the assistant analyzes a candidate's background, understands their career goals, and identifies job opportunities that closely match their profile.

Beyond simply listing vacancies, the assistant provides valuable insights into the current job market, helping users understand which skills employers are seeking and where they can improve to become stronger candidates.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to demonstrate how AI can support career development by combining profile analysis, intelligent job matching, and market insights into a single workflow.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

The assistant follows a three-step process:

1. **Profile Analysis**

   * Understands the user's professional background, technical skills, experience, certifications, and achievements.

2. **Career Preference Analysis**

   * Identifies the user's preferred roles, work style (Remote/Hybrid/Onsite), salary expectations, locations, and other job preferences.

3. **Intelligent Job Matching**

   * Searches for suitable opportunities, ranks them based on compatibility, filters out irrelevant listings, and presents the most relevant jobs along with career insights.

---

## 📊 Output

The assistant generates:

* A ranked list of matching job opportunities
* Company and role information
* Job locations and posting dates
* Direct application links
* Match scores with personalized explanations
* Salary (when available)
* Frequently requested skills
* Skill gap analysis
* Market demand insights
* Personalized recommendations to improve interview success

---

## 💡 Key Benefits

* Saves time during the job search process.
* Delivers personalized job recommendations instead of generic listings.
* Helps identify missing skills before applying.
* Provides insights into current hiring trends.
* Supports better career planning through data-driven recommendations.
